International Football Tournaments Analysis And Visualization

This project focuses on analyzing data from popular international football tournaments. It includes SQL queries to extract insights and build relational database. In addition, through the power of interactive visualization using Tableau, this project presents facts and trends from tournaments all around the globe. By exploring team performance, tournament and country statistics, home team win rate and goalscoring trend over time, the visualizations provide a comprehensive understanding of the data, enabling viewers to gain valuable insights into the world of football.

Vietnamese Car Price Prediction

This project utilizes machine learning algorithms to predict car prices based on relevant features such as brand, model, year, mileage, engine type, etc. By building and evaluating regression models, this project provides insights into the factors that influence car prices and offers a real-time prediction interface. The goal is to assist buyers, sellers, or car enthusiasts in making informed decisions regarding car pricing.
